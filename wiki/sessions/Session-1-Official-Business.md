# Session 1 - Official Business

**Transcript Source:** `Stars g1.md`

**In-World Date:** 143-1105, Wednesday. The Solomani Party Congress and internal hearing are expected on Friday.

**Present:** [[0-31 Saichi Maru (Annette)]], [[Cass Pien (Kolb)]], [[Ciernan Seanislav (Peter)]], [[Dan Araujo (Josh)]], [[Emma Colbert (Laura)]], [[Judith Dumont (Carla)]], [[Varian Svazz (Allen)]], [[Victor Mendes (Steinbach)]], [[Zoy (Damon)]]

## Synopsis

The crew were summoned to the [[Hall des Futurs Choisis]] in Nouvelle Sainte-Jeanne for official genetic, financial, military, or administrative review. The process seemed routine at first, but the grouping was suspicious: too many armed veterans and connected figures were gathered together, and several already knew one another through prior service.

While waiting, the group saw a news item about Minister [[Alain Mercier]], who had announced that he would reveal corruption in the genetic files at the coming Party Congress. Shortly afterward, Mercier rushed through the office area and was killed by gunfire. Several characters sensed or discovered that their own weapons had fired or moved without normal handling; Ciernan found one shotgun round missing and the weapon hot. The group quickly realized they were being framed.

[[Elena Verona]], a Social Solidarity commissar, arrived with Home Guard personnel and ordered everyone to stand down. At first many complied, but Emma's cybernetic/attached computer received evidence or warning that the authorities intended to kill them. She opened fire, turning the arrest into a fight. The group defeated the Home Guard detachment, left Verona alive but wounded/unconscious, looted critical gear, and fled while cameras recorded the incident.

The escape escalated when a walker/mech breached the window. [[0-31 Saichi Maru (Annette)]] killed the pilot without destroying the machine, letting the group use the walker to descend the exterior of the building. A driverless grav speeder then collected them and carried them through waste-disposal and service routes beneath their arcology.

The rescuer was [[Marcel Delphine]], an uplifted dolphin and leading figure of the [[Jardin Syndicate]]. Marcel claimed the Syndicate had originally feared Mercier had discovered their genetic-identity manipulation, but withdrew when they learned Mercier's evidence pointed "from above, not below." Marcel believes the frame-up will be used to blame both the player characters and the Syndicate while hiding the real corruption.

Marcel gave the group [[Identity Veils]] that can fool camera recognition for about three cycles, plus a one-use communicator. He offered the unedited assassination footage in exchange for future work. The group withdrew to Emma's safe warehouse and spent several hours gathering gear, contacting allies, checking the news, and planning next moves.

## Major Events

1. The player characters were summoned to the Hall for official review and arrived armed under military-ID allowances.
2. The waiting-room queue behaved oddly, implying the group had been deliberately assembled.
3. Mercier's public announcement about genetic corruption aired shortly before he passed through the office area.
4. Mercier was killed by impossible or remote-triggered shots apparently linked to the characters' weapons.
5. Social Solidarity and Home Guard forces moved to contain the scene.
6. Emma acted after receiving a warning through her attached computer; the group fought its way out.
7. The group looted Mercier's locked pad and phone, Home Guard weapons, and Verona's one-use grav belt.
8. Saichi neutralized a walker pilot, enabling the group to ride the walker down the building.
9. A driverless grav speeder brought the group into hidden service routes beneath the arcology.
10. Marcel Delphine revealed the Jardin Syndicate's interest in keeping the group alive.
11. News broadcasts immediately blamed "genetic half-breeds" and used fake footage of Zoy throwing a grenade.
12. The group identified several next options: clear their names, work with Marcel, seek forged documents, reach the Transstar legal safe zone, acquire a boat/submersible, repair a sunken ship-boat, or eventually get off-world toward [[Miasma]].

## Clues and Revelations

- Mercier's findings were not about Syndicate genetic-identity manipulation, according to Marcel; they allegedly pointed to corruption "from above."
- The characters were likely selected because their military backgrounds, weapons, and prior connections could be made to look like a rebel or assassin cell.
- The assassination footage was edited almost immediately into public propaganda.
- Social Solidarity is local internal security on Jardin; SolSec handles terrorism and external threats.
- The Jardin Syndicate forges or manipulates genetic identity records for people excluded by the system, including mixed-heritage or politically disfavored citizens.
- False genetic findings may be used against political enemies or their descendants.
- Hidden service spaces, cleaning routes, ventilation paths, and waste-disposal access ways form a criminal underlayer beneath Nouvelle Sainte-Jeanne.
- Marcel has unedited footage of the assassination but will not give it freely.
