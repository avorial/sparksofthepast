# Sunken Ship-Boat

## Overview

One of Cass Pien's smuggling contacts reported a broken ship-boat sunk under the water. If the group can acquire a naval vessel, boat, or submersible and reach it, they may be able to repair it and use it as part of an escape route.

## Session 1 Context

At the end of [[Session 1 - Official Business]], the group identified this as one of several possible paths forward:

- Acquire a boat or submersible.
- Reach the sunken craft.
- Repair it.
- Use it to move away from direct Jardin controls or set up a later off-world escape.

## Limits

- The ship-boat's condition is unknown.
- The group does not yet have the needed naval transport.
- It is not confirmed that the ship-boat can leave the planet or only help them reach another departure route.
