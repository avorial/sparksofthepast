# False News Broadcast

## Overview

Within hours of the [[Mercier Assassination]], public news reported a terrorist attack at the Hall of Continuity and claimed that Minister [[Alain Mercier]] had been killed by genetic "half-breeds." The report showed fake footage of [[Zoy (Damon)]] throwing a grenade at Mercier.

The broadcast also presented [[Elena Verona]] as a harmed witness and claimed the group killed Mercier's bodyguards.

## Confirmed False Elements

- Zoy did not throw a grenade at Mercier in the transcript.
- Mercier was killed by gunfire linked to the frame-up, not by a grenade.
- The story conceals the fact that the group's weapons appeared to be manipulated or spoofed.

## Uses

- Publicly brands the group as terrorists.
- Links the case to genetic purity politics.
- Gives authorities justification to hunt the group and possibly crack down on the [[Jardin Syndicate]] or other genetically disfavored populations.
