# Elena Kovacs

*Taj Mahal Class Purser*

## Overview

Elena Kovacs is a purser on [[Transstar]]'s Taj Mahal-class liner SS Nouvelle Aube, overseeing high-passage travel for billionaires, admirals, diplomats, and celebrities without breaking a sweat. She possesses enough overheard gossip to destabilize several governments, all of it gathered from passengers who assumed a purser wasn't listening. Her discretion is worth more to Transstar than the fares.

## Notable For

- Manages luxury high-passage aboard the Nouvelle Aube.
- Holds a fortune in overheard elite gossip.
- Her discretion is a corporate asset in itself.
