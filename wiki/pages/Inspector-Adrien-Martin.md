# Inspector Adrien Martin

*Social Solidarity Liaison*

## Overview

Inspector Adrien Martin is the [[Social Solidarity]] liaison for [[Quartier Sainte-Liberté]], responsible for maintaining relations between the district's organizations and the government. It is an extremely difficult job: he must keep the peace in a quarter where the [[Jardin Syndicate]], the merchants, and the data brokers all have interests the state would rather not acknowledge. Managing Sainte-Liberté means knowing which crimes to see and which to overlook.

## Notable For

- State's point of contact for a semi-lawless district.
- Balances Social Solidarity's demands against local realities.
- Survives by choosing which infractions to notice.
