# Sister Celeste Moreau

*Community Organizer*

## Overview

Sister Celeste Moreau runs charitable programs throughout the lower districts of the Rousseau Arcology and is widely respected by the residents she serves. Where the state measures citizens by genetic and demographic value, her work reaches the people the Perfection System ranks lowest, which makes her both beloved in the commons and quietly watched from above. Her networks in the depths of the arcology give her reach that official charity never manages.

## Notable For

- Operates charity programs across Rousseau's lower districts.
- Trusted by residents the state tends to overlook.
- Commands informal reach into the arcology's poorest levels.
