# SolSec

## Overview

SolSec is distinguished in the transcript from [[Social Solidarity]]. On Jardin, Social Solidarity functions as the local secret police and internal security arm, while SolSec handles terrorism and external threats.

[[Emma Colbert (Laura)]] has a SolSec background as a secret agent, which shapes how she thinks about cover, contacts, and state security.

## Session 2 - Blocked Investigation

During [[Session 2 - Masks and Markers]], [[Inspector Marc Valois]] attempted to gain access to the [[Mercier Assassination]] scene at the [[Hall des Futurs Choisis]]. [[Social Solidarity]] blocked him, arguing that SolSec could not be trusted because a SolSec operative was implicated in the assassination.

Emma believes her own SolSec connection may have been useful to the conspirators: by framing the group with a SolSec-linked operative, they created a reason to keep SolSec away from the evidence.
