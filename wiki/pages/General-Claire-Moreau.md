# General Claire Moreau

*Commander, Marine Forces Jardin*

## Overview

General Claire Moreau commands Marine Forces Jardin, responsible for all marine training and deployment operations run out of the [[Fort Robespierre Naval Annex]]. Widely respected by frontline personnel, she leads the ground and boarding forces that back the Navy's presence in the system. In a crisis that turned military, her marines would be the boots the Palace reached for.

## Notable For

- Commands Jardin's marine training and deployment.
- Respected by frontline troops.
- Controls the system's ground and boarding forces.
