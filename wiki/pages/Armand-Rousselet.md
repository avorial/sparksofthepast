# Armand Rousselet

*Senior Customs Liaison*

## Overview

Armand Rousselet is [[Transstar]]'s Senior Customs Liaison, working the seam between the company and Jardin's customs authorities. Thirty years of finding legal solutions to impossible problems have earned him the hatred of many smugglers and the secret respect of just as many. He is the man Transstar sends when a shipment's paperwork needs to become, quite legally, someone else's problem.

## Notable For

- Thirty years bridging Transstar and customs.
- Turns impossible shipments into legal ones.
- Hated and respected across the smuggling trade.
