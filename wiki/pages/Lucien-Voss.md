# Lucien Voss

*Senior Freight Broker*

## Overview

Lucien Voss is a senior freight broker and something of a legend along Broker's Row in the [[Transstar Sector Exchange]], capable of arranging transport to almost anywhere — for a price. For a party that needs to move cargo, people, or itself off Jardin without answering identity questions, a broker of Voss's reach is exactly the sort of contact worth cultivating. The price, of course, is never only money.

## Notable For

- Can arrange freight to nearly any destination.
- A legend of the Exchange's Broker's Row.
- A plausible route off-world for those who can pay.
