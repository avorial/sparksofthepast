# Mara Kovalenko

*Director of Foreign Trade Relations*

## Overview

Mara Kovalenko directs Foreign Trade Relations at the [[Transstar Sector Exchange]], managing its dealings with Imperial and Hive commercial interests. She holds one of the largest diplomatic contact networks in the subsector, the kind of address book that makes a person useful to intelligence services whether or not she works for them. Through her, the Exchange speaks to powers the Confederation prefers to keep at arm's length.

## Notable For

- Manages Imperial and Hive commercial relations.
- Holds a vast subsector-wide contact network.
- A natural conduit to — and target of — foreign interests.
