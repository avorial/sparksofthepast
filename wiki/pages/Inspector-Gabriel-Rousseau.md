# Inspector Gabriel Rousseau

*Senior Customs Officer*

## Overview

Inspector Gabriel Rousseau is a senior customs officer at [[Customs & Immigration Checkpoint Harmony]] and a legend among anti-smuggling investigators. He knows the tells of a forged identity and a doctored manifest the way other people know faces, which makes him the officer a smuggling operation most wants to avoid drawing. For travelers relying on [[Identity Veils]] or borrowed papers, Rousseau is the kind of examiner those tricks were not built to fool.

## Notable For

- Legendary anti-smuggling customs investigator.
- Reads forged identities and manifests on sight.
- The examiner false papers are least likely to survive.
