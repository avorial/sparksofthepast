# Colette Moreau

*President, Liberty Merchants Association*

## Overview

Colette Moreau presides over the Liberty Merchants Association and is one of the most influential civilian leaders in [[Quartier Sainte-Liberté]], the capital's neon-lit entertainment and data-market district. She speaks for the legitimate businesses that keep the quarter running, which in Sainte-Liberté means constant negotiation with the [[Jardin Syndicate]] presence that runs the rest of it. Her association is where the district's above-board and below-board economies quietly meet.

## Notable For

- Leads Sainte-Liberté's organized merchant interests.
- Brokers between legitimate trade and Syndicate influence.
- A civilian power center in a Syndicate-shadowed district.
