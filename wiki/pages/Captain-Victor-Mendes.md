# Captain Victor Mendes

*Senior Transstar Freight Captain*

## Overview

Captain Victor Mendes is a respected merchant captain known throughout the [[Port Sainte-Jeanne Downport]] community and a senior freight captain for [[Transstar]]. A former Confederation Navy petty officer, he now commands the freighter Prosperity's Promise and is trusted with sensitive cargo assignments precisely because of his reputation for reliability, honesty, and protecting his crew. He refuses to carry anything he believes is connected to slavery — a line that makes him useful to some and useless to others. (He is a separate person from the Traveller Victor Mendes.)

## Notable For

- Commands the freighter Prosperity's Promise.
- Trusted with sensitive cargo; former Navy petty officer.
- Will not transport anything tied to slavery.
