# Agent Luc Deschamps

*SolSec Liaison Officer*

## Overview

Agent Luc Deschamps is the [[SolSec]] liaison officer at [[Customs & Immigration Checkpoint Harmony]], officially responsible for interagency coordination and unofficially responsible for a good deal more. As the Solomani security service's eyes at the border, he watches for the terrorism and external threats that fall outside [[Social Solidarity]]'s planetary remit — a jurisdiction that, since the Mercier frame-up, now brushes uncomfortably close to the party.

## Notable For

- SolSec's presence at the arrival checkpoint.
- Watches for external and terrorism-linked threats.
- His remit now overlaps the party's framed status.
