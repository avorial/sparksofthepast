# Director Luc Beaumont

*Port Authority Director*

## Overview

Director Luc Beaumont runs the Port Authority at [[Port Sainte-Jeanne Downport]], overseeing the largest transportation facility on Jardin. Pragmatic and politically skilled, he keeps cargo, passengers, and naval traffic moving through the world's primary gateway while managing the competing demands of customs, the Navy, and [[Social Solidarity]]. Everything that enters or leaves Jardin by the front door passes, in some sense, through his office.

## Notable For

- Runs Jardin's primary downport.
- Balances customs, naval, and security interests.
- Controls the world's main point of arrival and departure.
