# Secteur d'Échange Transstar
# Transstar Sector Exchange

*"The Market Never Sleeps."*

**Location:** Grand Axis District, Nouvelle Sainte-Jeanne, Jardin

**Type:** Special Economic Zone

**Administrative Authority:** Transstar Economic Directorate

**Status:** Semi-Autonomous Economic District

**Population:** 2.1 Million Permanent Residents

**Daily Population:** 12-15 Million

**Primary Functions:**
- Interstellar Trade
- Banking
- Freight Brokerage
- Corporate Headquarters
- Commodity Markets
- Foreign Investment
- Arbitration Courts

**Nickname:** The Exchange

---

# Overview

The **Transstar Sector Exchange** is the economic heart of Jardin and one of the most important financial centers in the Solomani Front.

Located at the southern end of the Grand Axis between Rousseau Arcology and Port Sainte-Jeanne, the district serves as the central hub for commerce, banking, shipping contracts, insurance markets, and interstellar investment throughout the subsector.

Unlike most of Jardin, the Exchange operates under a special legal charter negotiated between Transstar, the Supreme Presidency, and the Solomani Party.

The result is a district that feels markedly different from the rest of the world.

Visitors often compare it to:

- A corporate city-state
- A permanent trade conference
- A civilized battlefield

The district is simultaneously one of the wealthiest and least ideological locations on Jardin.

Money speaks every language.

---

# History

## Foundation

The Exchange was established shortly after Jardin became the dominant economic power of the subsector.

As trade expanded, government officials recognized that traditional Party bureaucracy could not process commercial activity quickly enough.

Special exemptions were granted.

Corporate courts were established.

Foreign companies were invited.

Certain regulations were relaxed.

The experiment proved wildly successful.

---

## The Great Expansion

Following the Solomani Rim War, the Exchange became one of the Confederation's most important frontier trade hubs.

Merchants operating between:

- Confederation Worlds
- Imperial Border Systems
- Hive Federation Markets
- Frontier Colonies

all found themselves passing through Jardin.

Transstar aggressively expanded the district.

Within decades it became the largest concentration of commercial activity in the entire subsector.

---

# Governance

The Exchange exists under a special charter.

It is not independent.

But it is not fully governed by standard Jardinese law.

The arrangement remains controversial.

Officially:

- The Supreme Presidency governs the district.
- Confederation law applies.
- Social Solidarity retains authority.

In practice:

Transstar runs most day-to-day operations.

---

# The Exchange Authority

The district is administered by the **Exchange Authority**, a joint body consisting of:

- Transstar Executives
- Party Representatives
- Economic Ministry Officials
- Selected Corporate Delegates

Most decisions are made through negotiation.

---

# Exchange Security Bureau

## Corporate Police

The district's police force.

Often called:

> "Blue Jackets"

The Bureau handles:

- Financial crimes
- Commercial disputes
- Smuggling investigations
- Contract enforcement
- Corporate security

Uniforms are dark blue with silver trim.

Their authority exists only within Exchange territory.

Outside the district they possess no special privileges.

Inside the district they are feared.

---

# Relationship with Social Solidarity

The relationship is tense.

Social Solidarity considers the Exchange dangerously independent.

The Exchange Authority considers Social Solidarity dangerously intrusive.

Both cooperate when necessary.

Neither trusts the other.

---

# Relationship with SolSec

SolSec maintains a permanent office inside the Exchange.

Officially:

Counter-espionage.

Unofficially:

Everyone knows the district is crawling with intelligence services.

---

# Architecture

The district combines:

- Corporate Arcologies
- Financial Towers
- Luxury Hotels
- Trade Centers
- Elevated Transit Systems

The skyline differs dramatically from the monumental government architecture found elsewhere in Nouvelle Sainte-Jeanne.

Corporate logos are everywhere.

Many buildings are illuminated twenty-four hours a day.

The Exchange never truly sleeps.

---

# Major Districts

## The Grand Market

The commercial center.

Contains:

- Commodity exchanges
- Trade houses
- Shipping brokers
- Merchant guild offices

Thousands of contracts worth billions of credits are signed here daily.

---

## Transstar Plaza

The symbolic center of the district.

Dominated by the massive **Transstar Solomani Front Headquarters**.

The building houses:

- Regional Management
- Route Development Offices
- Freight Coordination Centers
- Political Liaison Offices

Many citizens consider it the true center of power in the subsector.

---

## Meridian Financial Quarter

Home to:

- Banks
- Investment Houses
- Insurance Syndicates
- Commodity Funds

The wealthiest square kilometer on Jardin.

---

## International Concourse

The most cosmopolitan district on the planet.

Visitors include:

- Imperial merchants
- Hive traders
- Aslan brokers
- Independent captains

The district feels noticeably less ideological than the rest of Jardin.

Business comes first.

---

## Broker's Row

Hundreds of freight brokers compete for contracts.

Deals ranging from:

- Single cargo containers
- Entire colony shipments
- Naval procurement contracts

change hands here.

---

# Commerce

Nearly forty percent of all legal interstellar commerce entering Jardin passes through the Exchange.

Major industries include:

- Shipping
- Finance
- Insurance
- Logistics
- Data Analysis
- Commodity Trading

The district generates enormous tax revenue.

Many Party officials privately dislike the Exchange.

Few are willing to damage something so profitable.

---

# Foreign Presence

Special permits allow limited foreign corporate representation.

Offices from:

- Imperial Trading Houses
- Hive Federation Companies
- Independent Merchant Leagues

can be found throughout the district.

The Exchange remains one of the few places on Jardin where foreigners are common.

---

# Criminal Activity

Money attracts crime.

The Exchange hosts:

- Corporate espionage
- Insider trading
- Data theft
- Smuggling
- Financial fraud

Most activity remains hidden beneath layers of legality.

---

# The Jardin Syndicate

The Syndicate maintains extensive influence throughout the Exchange.

Activities include:

- Money laundering
- False cargo manifests
- Identity creation
- Smuggling services

Many Exchange Security officers spend entire careers fighting Syndicate operations.

Most achieve only temporary victories.

---

# Notable Individuals

## Antoine Mercier

**Executive Director, Solomani Front Operations**

The most powerful Transstar executive on Jardin.

Frequently rumored to possess more influence than several planetary ministers.

---

## Isabelle Fournier

**Chief Commissioner, Exchange Security Bureau**

Respected, feared, and incorruptible.

At least according to official statements.

---

## Lucien Voss

**Senior Freight Broker**

A legend within the district.

Capable of arranging transport to almost anywhere.

For a price.

---

## Mara Kovalenko

**Director of Foreign Trade Relations**

Responsible for managing interactions with Imperial and Hive commercial interests.

Possesses one of the largest diplomatic contact networks in the subsector.

---

# Public Reputation

Citizens of Jardin often hold mixed opinions regarding the Exchange.

Supporters call it:

> The Engine of Prosperity

Critics call it:

> The Capitalist Exception

Most simply call it:

> Where the Money Is.

---

## Session 1 - Potential Refuge

Emma's smuggler contact identified the Transstar Sector Exchange as a possible refuge after the [[Mercier Frame-Up]]. The Exchange is described as an independent legal entity inside Jardin: not fully outside local reality, but separate enough that the group may not be direct enemies of the state while inside it.

The contact compared it to a corporate enclave or embassy-like safe zone. If the [[Identity Veils]] begin to fail, reaching the Exchange may allow the group to speak more openly with contacts and seek corporate or smuggling options.

## Session 2 - Ammunition Lead

During [[Session 2 - Masks and Markers]], [[Lucin]] claimed he has a broker in Transstar who can provide military-grade ammunition if the group supplies the weapon models and credits. The GM also flagged Transstar as a safer next destination where the party might pick up ammunition and avoid being ambushed in the warehouse district.

# Referee Notes

The Transstar Sector Exchange should feel fundamentally different from the rest of Jardin.

The Palace of Continuity is about power.

The Hall of Chosen Futures is about control.

The Closed Gardens are about science.

Rousseau is about people.

The Exchange is about money.

Here ideology bends around profit.

Party officials, merchants, smugglers, bankers, intelligence agents, and foreign traders all operate in close proximity.

It is the one place on Jardin where a person can genuinely believe that credits matter more than politics.

Whether that belief is true remains open to debate.


GM-only truth goes here.
:::
