# Nadia Giroux

*Investigative Journalist*

## Overview

Nadia Giroux is an investigative journalist working out of the Rousseau Arcology, known for exposing corruption and embarrassing powerful people who assumed their arrangements were private. She receives anonymous threats frequently and ignores them just as frequently. On a world where [[Social Solidarity]] curates the official narrative, a reporter who publishes uncomfortable truths is both a rare asset and a standing liability, and her continued freedom to work says as much about who protects her as about her nerve.

## Notable For

- Exposes corruption among Rousseau's powerful.
- Receives — and ignores — regular anonymous threats.
- A potential source or ally for anyone investigating official cover-ups.
