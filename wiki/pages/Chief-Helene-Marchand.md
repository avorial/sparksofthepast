# Chief Helene Marchand

## Overview

Chief Helene Marchand is identified by a gold plaque as Chief of Staff of the President. During [[Session 2 - Masks and Markers]], the group delivered Lucin's crate to a secured residence bearing her name.

## Session 2

The group arrived at Marchand's address on Lucin's trike while wearing [[Identity Veils]]. Security personnel waved them through. A cybernetically enhanced butler accepted the crate, opened it with a code, released gas from the seal, and let a corgi out from inside.

The group left without challenging the delivery or asking questions on site.

## Open Questions

- Did Marchand personally request the delivery?
- Why was the crate routed through Lucin rather than normal official channels?
- Was the corgi genetically significant, politically sensitive, or simply contraband luxury?
- Does Marchand have ties to the underworld, the Closed Gardens, or the genetic research scandal?
