# Friday Party Congress Hearing

## Overview

The Friday Party Congress hearing is expected two days after the [[Mercier Assassination]]. [[Alain Mercier]] had planned to reveal his findings there before he was killed.

[[Cass Pien (Kolb)]] contacted a trusted ally to listen from inside the hearing and report who speaks, what story is told, and which political players are involved.

## Why It Matters

- Mercier's intended presentation may still shape the agenda.
- The official story may reveal who benefits from the [[Mercier Frame-Up]].
- Names and arguments raised at the hearing could point toward the faction or individual who silenced Mercier.

## Open Questions

- Will Mercier's actual findings be suppressed, altered, or replaced?
- Who will speak for the official investigation?
- Will the [[Jardin Syndicate]] be blamed directly?
- Will the player characters be publicly named as an assassin cell?
