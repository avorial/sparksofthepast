# Session 2 - Masks and Markers

**Transcript Source:** `transcript_2026-07-14T13_30_07.150Z.json`

## Synopsis

The group wakes in Emma's warehouse with practical problems: [[0-31 Saichi Maru (Annette)]] needs a heavy charge, the [[Identity Veils]] have little time left, and the party still needs evidence and supplies. They split up while their veils still hide them from surveillance.

At the [[Hall des Futurs Choisis]], the recon team sees [[Social Solidarity]] denying [[SolSec]] access to the assassination investigation. Emma recognizes [[Inspector Marc Valois]], a SolSec investigator whose work touches genetic crime and alien biology. Inside, the team intercepts a [[Mobile Data Tube]] from [[Dr. Luc Moreau]]. Its data says Mercier was killed for information passed to him.

Saichi recharges from a police mech during a public shaming at the Grand Axis. The supply team buys survival gear, creates modified burner comms, and meets [[Lucin]], a data broker. Lucin trades them a trike and future goodwill for a daylight delivery from Dome 2 of [[Les Jardins Fermés]] to [[Chief Helene Marchand]]'s secured residence. The crate contains a corgi.

Back at the warehouse, the recovered data points to long-term genetic-marker research tied to extraordinary or psionic-like effects. The file is corrupted before its final conclusion, but the findings may explain why Mercier was killed and may connect to the impossible bullet behavior in his assassination.

## What Happened

Game 2 opened the morning after the frame-up, with the party hidden in Emma's warehouse and running out of easy options. [[0-31 Saichi Maru (Annette)]] needed a heavy recharge, the [[Identity Veils]] had limited time left, and the group still lacked proof of who killed Mercier or why. The party split into teams to investigate the Hall, recharge Saichi, and gather supplies.

At the [[Hall des Futurs Choisis]], the recon team found [[Social Solidarity]] blocking [[SolSec]] from entering the assassination scene. [[Emma Colbert (Laura)]] recognized [[Inspector Marc Valois]], a SolSec investigator connected to genetic crimes and alien biology cases. Social Solidarity argued that SolSec could not be trusted because a SolSec operative had been implicated in the murder, making Emma's identity part of the political blockade.

Inside the Hall, the team followed [[Dr. Luc Moreau]], a nervous scientist who tried to flush a self-moving [[Mobile Data Tube]] through the bathroom plumbing. [[Zoy (Damon)]] and [[Cass Pien (Kolb)]] recovered the tube before it escaped, and Emma disarmed its self-destruct charge. The message attached to the courier said Mercier had obviously been killed for data that had been passed to him.

Elsewhere, Saichi and [[Varian Svazz (Allen)]] used a public shaming at the Grand Axis as cover to recharge from a police mech. The supply team went into [[Quartier Sainte-Liberté]], bought food, medical supplies, water containers, hygiene goods, batteries, and burner phones, and modified the phones into secure short-range comms. They also met [[Lucin]], an armored data broker with a cybernetic third arm, who offered them a trike and future goodwill in exchange for a daylight delivery.

The party took Lucin's job while the veils still worked. They picked up a golf-bag-sized crate from Dome 2 of [[Les Jardins Fermés]] and delivered it to a secured residence marked for [[Chief Helene Marchand]], Chief of Staff of the President. A cybernetic butler opened the crate with a code and released a corgi. The party left without incident and kept Lucin's trike.

Back at the warehouse, Emma and Zoy reviewed the recovered data chip. The file contained decades of genetic-marker research tied to extraordinary or psionic-like effects, including examples of people predicting random outputs or identifying hidden cards. The data was corrupted before its final conclusion, but the markers HX-14, SC-9, MY22, and PX-4 may explain why Mercier was killed and may connect to the impossible weapon behavior during the assassination.
