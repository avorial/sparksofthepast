## Overview

Marcel Delphine is believed to be the leader of the Jardin Syndicate, one of the largest criminal organizations operating within the Jardin Subsector. To SolSec he is a trafficker, smuggler, forger, and criminal mastermind. To thousands of refugees, migrants, and political undesirables, he is a savior who provided opportunities that governments denied.

An uplifted dolphin of advanced age, Delphine is rarely seen in public. When he does appear, he does so within a custom gravitic life-support frame that resembles a floating throne draped in deep blue robes embroidered with ancient Terran symbols.

Delphine presents himself not as a crime lord but as a social philosopher. He openly argues that governments have become obsessed with managing populations rather than helping individuals.

His most famous saying is:

> "A government plans futures. I help people choose them."

No public evidence has ever connected him directly to criminal activities, yet nearly every intelligence agency in the sector believes he sits at the center of a vast network of smugglers, corrupt officials, transport companies, and identity brokers.

Many believe he is the most dangerous man on Jardin.

Many others believe he is the most compassionate.

## Operational History

**1091:** First confirmed appearance in immigration records on Jardin.

**1094:** Establishes Delphine Relocation Services.

**1098:** Linked to refugee transport operations throughout the subsector.

**1102:** SolSec formally identifies the Jardin Syndicate as an organized criminal enterprise.

**1104:** Multiple anti-Syndicate investigations collapse after key witnesses disappear.

**1105:** Expands operations into Imperial border regions.

**1107:** Rumored to have negotiated agreements with several local political officials regarding refugee populations.

**1110:** Publicly funds the construction of three migrant assistance centers while simultaneously becoming the primary target of an interagency SolSec task force.

**1112:** Current activities unknown.
