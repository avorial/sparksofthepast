# Director Elise Fournier

*Administrator of District 14*

## Overview

Director Elise Fournier administers [[Crèche District 14]], one of the state creche districts where Jardin raises children communally under the Perfection System. Widely respected and known across the world as "the Mother of a Million," she embodies the system's gentlest and most sincerely believed face. That a single administrator can be a household name says how central state child-rearing is to Jardin's idea of itself. (She shares a surname, not an identity, with the Gardens botanist and the Social Solidarity director-general.)

## Notable For

- Administers a full state creche district.
- Known planet-wide as "the Mother of a Million."
- The sympathetic public face of the Perfection System.
