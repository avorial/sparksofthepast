# Mercier's Locked Pad

## Overview

Mercier's locked pad was recovered from [[Alain Mercier]] after his assassination. It is roughly Kindle-sized and password protected.

## Known Facts

- The pad was on Mercier's body.
- An attempted hack failed.
- Four attempts remain before the pad wipes itself.
- Its contents are unknown.
- At the end of [[Session 2 - Masks and Markers]], the group still intends to revisit the pad, but has not opened it.

## Importance

The pad may contain evidence of the genetic-file corruption Mercier intended to reveal at the Party Congress, or related notes about the people who silenced him.
