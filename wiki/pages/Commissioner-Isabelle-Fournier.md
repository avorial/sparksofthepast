# Commissioner Isabelle Fournier

*Chief Commissioner, Exchange Security Bureau*

## Overview

Commissioner Isabelle Fournier heads the Exchange Security Bureau, the corporate police force of the [[Transstar Sector Exchange]]. She is respected, feared, and — according to official statements — incorruptible. The Exchange is an independent legal entity where Confederation, Imperial, and Hive money all changes hands, so the person who polices its floor holds a quiet leverage over everyone who trades there. (She is not the medical botanist of the same surname at the Closed Gardens.)

## Notable For

- Commands the Exchange's corporate police.
- Officially incorruptible; universally feared.
- Polices a trading floor where three powers' money meets.
