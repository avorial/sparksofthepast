# Luc Girard

*Labor Coordinator*

## Overview

Luc Girard is [[Transstar]]'s Labor Coordinator, assigning thousands of dockworkers and logistics personnel across the Rousseau industrial levels. Officially he manages staffing; unofficially he can make careers appear or disappear, and his influence among working-class Party members is considerable. In a company town, the man who controls the shifts controls a great deal more than the schedule.

## Notable For

- Assigns thousands of dock and logistics workers.
- Can quietly make or break careers.
- Holds real sway among working-class Party members.
