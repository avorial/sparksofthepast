# Inspector Marc Valois

## Overview

Inspector Marc Valois is a [[SolSec]] investigator seen outside the [[Hall des Futurs Choisis]] during [[Session 2 - Masks and Markers]]. [[Emma Colbert (Laura)]] recognized him from prior SolSec circles, though he was not in her direct chain of command.

Valois works cases involving corrupt alien biology and genetic crimes. Because [[Alain Mercier]] was killed at the Hall while preparing to reveal genetic-file corruption, Valois believed the assassination connected to his own investigation.

## Session 2

Valois was arguing with [[Social Solidarity]] personnel outside the Hall. He demanded access to evidence, saying SolSec had the right to investigate because the matter involved a SolSec member and genetic crime. Social Solidarity refused, arguing that Mercier was a planetary official killed in a planetary government building and that a SolSec operative was implicated, so SolSec could not be trusted with access.

## Importance

Valois may be a route to bypass planetary security, but contacting him is risky. If he is honest, he may want the same evidence the party now holds. If he is watched, any approach could expose the group.
