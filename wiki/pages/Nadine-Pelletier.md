# Nadine Pelletier

*Passenger Services Director*

## Overview

Nadine Pelletier is [[Transstar]]'s Passenger Services Director and the public face of the line's luxury travel, overseeing the arrival of VIPs, Party officials, and wealthy travelers at the Jardin Orbital Terminal. Charismatic and media-savvy, she manages the experience of the powerful as they move through Jardin. Rumor holds that she knows every secret carried by every important passenger — a currency worth more than any fare. (No relation to Governor Adrien Pelletier of Rousseau.)

## Notable For

- Oversees VIP and Party-official travel.
- Charismatic, media-trained public face of Transstar luxury.
- Said to know every important passenger's secrets.
