# Commissioner Elise Martin

*Directorate of Citizen Integrity*

## Overview

Commissioner Elise Martin coordinates [[Social Solidarity]] operations within [[Customs & Immigration Checkpoint Harmony]] through its Directorate of Citizen Integrity. Where the customs officers watch cargo and papers, Martin's people watch for ideological and genetic irregularity — the softer, more political layer of the border. An arrival that clears customs cleanly can still be quietly marked by her directorate.

## Notable For

- Runs Social Solidarity's presence at the checkpoint.
- Screens arrivals for ideological and genetic flags.
- Can mark a traveler who otherwise cleared customs.
