# Timeline

## 143-1105 - Official Business

### Summons to the Hall

The player characters are summoned to the [[Hall des Futurs Choisis]] in Nouvelle Sainte-Jeanne for official genetic, military, financial, or AI-status review. Their military IDs allow many of them to enter armed, but the grouping is suspiciously convenient for a later frame-up.

### Mercier Broadcast

A news item reports that [[Alain Mercier]], Minister of Genetics, plans to reveal corruption in the genetic-affairs files at the coming Party Congress.

### Mercier Assassination

Mercier rushes through the office area and is killed by multiple shots. Several characters detect signs that their own weapons have moved or fired without normal handling. The group realizes they may have been framed.

### Social Solidarity Response

[[Elena Verona]] and [[Home Guard]] personnel attempt to contain the room. Emma receives a warning through her attached computer system that the authorities intend to kill the group. The confrontation turns into a firefight.

### Escape From the Hall

The group defeats the immediate security force, loots key equipment, and escapes when [[0-31 Saichi Maru (Annette)]] kills the pilot of an attacking walker/mech. The group rides the walker down the outside of the building and is collected by a driverless grav speeder.

### Marcel Delphine

The grav speeder carries the group through hidden service routes beneath [[Arcosphere Rousseau]] to [[Marcel Delphine]]. Marcel gives them [[Identity Veils]], explains the [[Jardin Syndicate]]'s interest, and claims the real corruption Mercier found came "from above."

### Aftermath

The official story becomes the [[Mercier Frame-Up]]. News broadcasts falsely blame genetic outcasts and show fabricated footage of [[Zoy (Damon)]] throwing a grenade. The group regroups in Emma's safe warehouse, gathers gear, contacts allies, and considers escape or investigation options.

### Open Paths

- Investigate who silenced Mercier.
- Obtain the Syndicate's unedited footage.
- Unlock [[Mercier's Locked Pad]].
- Reach the [[Transstar Sector Exchange]].
- Acquire forged identities.
- Find a boat/submersible and repair the [[Sunken Ship-Boat]].
- Move within the Jardin system before attempting off-world escape to [[Miasma]] or elsewhere.
