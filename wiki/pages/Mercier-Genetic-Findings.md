# Mercier Genetic Findings

## Overview

The Mercier genetic findings are recovered research notes from the [[Mobile Data Tube]] intercepted in [[Session 2 - Masks and Markers]]. The data appears to be part of what [[Alain Mercier]] planned to reveal before his assassination.

The notes describe roughly fifty years of genetic tests and documentation. The file is corrupted near the end, so the final conclusion is missing.

## Extraordinary Effects

The data correlates multiple genetic markers with unusual, possibly psionic or otherwise enhanced effects. Known examples from the file include:

- Subject 18 predicted a random matter generator 214 consecutive times.
- Subject 21 consistently identified concealed playing cards.
- Other references imply remote perception, precognition, or similarly abnormal cognition, but the transcript does not preserve every detail.

## Marker Notes

Laura's science review identified the following:

- **HX-14:** Unequivocally alien genetic material; not uplifted Terran animal origin.
- **SC-9:** Solomani genetic-code marker and a passable gene within Solomani genetic records.
- **MY22:** A duplicated genetic frailty or error, the sort of thing normally treated as a defect.
- **PX-4:** Non-Solomani genetic material; possibly another human lineage rather than fully alien.

Ciernan compared the notes to his own genetic papers. He does not have the identified markers.

## Why It Matters

The data may explain why Mercier was killed. It may also give the party a possible explanation for the impossible behavior of the bullets during the assassination: the conspiracy may involve psionic, genetic, or exotic biological technology rather than simple remote weapon control.

## Open Questions

- Do any of the player characters have these markers?
- Are the official genetic records trustworthy?
- Who ran the fifty-year testing program?
- Were Mercier's findings hidden because they undermine Jardin's ideology, because they expose alien inheritance, or because they reveal useful abilities?
- Is the corrupted ending recoverable from another source?
- Did the weapon manipulation depend on these markers?
