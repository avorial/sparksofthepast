# Dr. Amelie Rousseau

*Chief Developmental Psychologist*

## Overview

Dr. Amelie Rousseau is Chief Developmental Psychologist for [[Crèche District 14]], and her research shapes educational policy across Jardin. She studies how the state's communally raised children develop, and her findings feed directly back into how the next cohort is taught. In a system that treats child-rearing as an engineering problem, she is one of the engineers.

## Notable For

- Her research drives Jardin-wide educational policy.
- Studies the outcomes of communal child-rearing.
- Helps engineer how each new cohort is raised.
