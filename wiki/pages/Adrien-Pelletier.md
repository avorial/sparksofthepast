# Adrien Pelletier

*Governor of Rousseau*

## Overview

Adrien Pelletier governs the Arcosphère Rousseau, the largest single structure on Jardin and home to nearly two billion permanent residents. A dedicated administrator, he genuinely believes the arcology is a working model of humanity's future and defends the Solomani planning ideal in every public forum. Running a self-contained city of that scale means his authority touches housing, transit, industry, and security in ways that rival a planetary minister, and critics note that few decisions inside Rousseau happen without his office knowing.

## Notable For

- Governs a residential megastructure of roughly 1.8 billion residents.
- Treats the arcology as proof that Solomani social planning succeeds.
- Holds practical power over Rousseau comparable to a planetary minister.
