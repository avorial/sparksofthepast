# Port Sainte-Jeanne Downport

*"Gateway to the Frontier."*

**Location:** Western Nouvelle Sainte-Jeanne

**Type:** Class A Starport Downport

**Administrative Authority:** Port Authority of Jardin

**Operational Control:** Transstar Port Services

**Security:** Port Security Directorate, Customs Bureau, Social Solidarity, SolSec Liaison Office

**Population:** 3.5 Million Permanent Residents

**Daily Population:** 10-20 Million

**Nickname:** The Port

---

# Overview

The **Port Sainte-Jeanne Downport** is the primary surface gateway to Jardin and one of the busiest starports in the Solomani Front.

Every day thousands of passengers and millions of tons of cargo pass through its terminals.

The Port serves as the physical connection between:

- Jardin
- The Solomani Confederation
- The Imperial Border
- The Hive Federation
- The Frontier Worlds

Most visitors see the Palace of Continuity as the symbol of Jardin.

Most residents know the truth.

The Port keeps the world alive.

---

# First Impressions

The first thing visitors notice is the scale.

The second is the noise.

The third is the smell.

Fuel.

Industrial lubricants.

Imported foods.

 Ocean air.

Humanity.

The Port never truly sleeps.

Ships arrive at all hours.

Cargo moves continuously.

Announcements echo from towering terminals.

Some visitors love it immediately.

Others never feel comfortable here.

---

# History

## The Original Landing Field

The first colonial settlement on Jardin was established near the present-day Port.

Its founders were:

- Entrepreneurs
- Soldiers
- Adventurers
- Political dissidents

Many arrived specifically to escape Imperial authority.

Their descendants still celebrate this history.

---

## The Expansion Era

As Jardin built its early empire, the Port expanded dramatically.

Military shipyards.

Colonial fleets.

 Trade convoys.

Occupation forces.

All departed from Port Sainte-Jeanne.

Some historians argue the Port was the true heart of the old Jardinese Empire.

---

## Modern Era

Today the Port remains one of the largest transportation hubs in the subsector.

Its facilities support:

- Commercial traffic
- Naval logistics
- Colonial development
- Diplomatic missions
- Frontier exploration

---

# Layout

The Port covers hundreds of square kilometers.

Major sections include:

## Arrival Terminal Complex

Passenger processing center.

Most visitors begin their journey here.

Contains:

- Customs
- Immigration
- Transportation links
- Hotels
- Currency exchanges

The terminal handles millions of travelers every month.

---

## Cargo Basin Alpha

The largest freight handling district.

Dominated by:

- Warehouses
- Cargo cranes
- Freight towers
- Container yards

Thousands of workers operate here around the clock.

---

## Colonial Transit Zone

Specialized facilities serving:

- Frontier Colonies
- Development Projects
- Settlement Programs

Many citizens beginning new lives pass through this district.

It has become known as:

> The District of Second Chances

---

## Orbital Elevator Complex

A massive transportation system linking the downport to Port Sainte-Jeanne Highport.

The elevators operate continuously.

Most cargo reaching orbit passes through these towers.

---

## Naval Interface Sector

Joint facilities supporting:

- Confederation Navy
- Home Forces
- Military Logistics

Public access is heavily restricted.

---

# The Port Authority

The Port is administered by the **Port Authority of Jardin**.

Responsibilities include:

- Traffic Control
- Safety Enforcement
- Infrastructure Management
- Customs Coordination

The Authority works closely with:

- Transstar
- Social Solidarity
- SolSec
- Confederation Navy

---

# Security

The Port contains more law enforcement personnel than any other location on Jardin.

Security organizations include:

## Port Security Directorate

Primary law enforcement agency.

Handles:

- Smuggling
- Violent crime
- Port security
- Cargo theft

---

## Customs Bureau

Responsible for:

- Import controls
- Export inspections
- Contraband interdiction

Many traders fear Customs more than Port Security.

---

## Social Solidarity

Monitors:

- Returning citizens
- Immigration concerns
- Ideological risks

Visitors often encounter them without realizing it.

---

## SolSec Liaison Office

Handles:

- Foreign intelligence threats
- Espionage
- Counter-terrorism

Officially a small office.

Nobody believes this.

---

# Culture

The Port is one of the least typical places on Jardin.

People from across Charted Space pass through daily.

Visitors include:

- Merchants
- Colonists
- Diplomats
- Mercenaries
- Explorers
- Refugees

As a result the Port possesses a reputation for being unusually open-minded.

At least by Jardinese standards.

---

# Neighborhoods

## The Sailor's Quarter

The oldest district in the Port.

Filled with:

- Taverns
- Boarding Houses
- Restaurants
- Entertainment Venues

A favorite destination for ship crews.

---

## Republic Market

A sprawling commercial bazaar.

Goods from hundreds of worlds can be found here.

Many items are legal.

Most are.

---

## Founder's Row

Historic district preserving buildings from the original colony.

Popular with tourists.

---

## The Freightlands

Industrial district serving cargo workers.

A rough area after dark.

---

# Organized Crime

The Port has always attracted criminal organizations.

Common activities include:

- Smuggling
- Cargo theft
- Identity fraud
- Illegal migration
- Contraband trafficking

The Jardin Syndicate maintains significant influence throughout the Port.

---

# The Syndicate Presence

The Port serves as one of the Syndicate's most important operational zones.

Common activities include:

- Forged travel documents
- Unofficial shipping services
- Labor rackets
- Black-market logistics

Port Security fights a constant battle against them.

Neither side expects victory.
