# Antoine Mercier

*Executive Director, Solomani Front Operations*

## Overview

Antoine Mercier is Executive Director of Solomani Front Operations for [[Transstar]] and the most powerful Transstar executive on Jardin. He is frequently rumored to wield more real influence than several planetary ministers, which on a world this centralized is a striking thing to say about a corporate officer. Whether his surname ties him to the late Minister of Genetics is not established, but in a city this tangled the coincidence rarely goes unremarked.

## Notable For

- Most powerful Transstar executive on Jardin.
- Said to outweigh several planetary ministers in influence.
- Surname link to Alain Mercier is unconfirmed.
