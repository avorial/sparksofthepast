# Lucin

## Overview

Lucin is an armored data broker encountered in [[Quartier-Sainte-Liberte]] during [[Session 2 - Masks and Markers]]. He operated from a dark alley market where credits were exchanged for data chips.

Lucin wore armor and had a cybernetic third arm emerging from his back. He was cautious at first, but Dan and the supply team opened a working relationship.

## Known Services

- Data brokerage.
- Possible transport contacts.
- Access to an ammunition broker in [[Transstar Sector Exchange]].
- Underworld errands and trust-building jobs.

## Session 2 Deal

Lucin offered the group a trike if they picked up a 36-by-20-ish crate from Dome 2 of [[Les Jardins Fermes]] and delivered it during daylight to an address he provided. The group completed the errand and discovered the destination was the secured residence of [[Chief Helene Marchand]]. The crate contained a corgi.

The trike is not immediately reported stolen; Lucin said the people who bought it will not realize one vehicle is missing from a larger lot for months or years.
