## Overview

The **Customs & Immigration Checkpoint Harmony** is the primary entry point into Jardin.

Every traveler entering the planet through Port Sainte-Jeanne eventually passes through its halls.

For most visitors the experience is efficient, professional, and surprisingly polite.

For others it is one of the most stressful experiences of their lives.

The checkpoint serves as the intersection between:

- Social Solidarity
- Customs Authority
- SolSec
- Transstar
- The Hall of Chosen Futures

Every traveler is recorded.

Every declaration is logged.

Every arrival becomes part of the system.

As many Jardinese officials proudly state:

> "A secure society begins with knowing who enters it."

---

## First Impressions

The checkpoint resembles a modern airport terminal scaled to impossible proportions.

Visitors encounter:

- Massive arrival halls
- Automated processing systems
- Biometric scanners
- Citizen service kiosks
- Customs inspection zones

The atmosphere is calm.

Almost too calm.

Announcements are soft.

Queues move quickly.

Officers remain polite.

Nobody raises their voice.

Yet every traveler senses they are being observed.

Because they are.

---

## Architecture

The facility was intentionally designed to project confidence rather than intimidation.

Features include:

- White stone architecture
- Indoor gardens
- Natural lighting systems
- Public art displays
- Quiet processing areas

Government planners wanted arrivals to feel welcomed.

The extensive security measures remain largely hidden from view.

---

## Primary Divisions

### Immigration Processing Hall

The largest section of the facility.

Handles:

- Visitor entry permits
- Residency applications
- Work authorizations
- Colonial transfers

Most travelers spend less than thirty minutes here.

Most.

---

### Citizen Services Bureau

Dedicated to Jardinese citizens.

Provides:

- Identity updates
- Travel registrations
- Repatriation services
- Documentation support

Returning citizens are often directed here before being allowed to proceed.

---

### Customs Inspection Complex

Responsible for:

- Cargo inspections
- Contraband detection
- Duty collection
- Import certification

The Customs Bureau possesses a reputation for thoroughness.

Smugglers possess other descriptions.

---

### Biosecurity Division

Protects Jardin from:

- Disease vectors
- Agricultural contamination
- Biological hazards

The division maintains quarantine facilities capable of isolating thousands of travelers.

---

### Visitor Orientation Center

Provides information regarding:

- Local laws
- Social expectations
- Cultural customs
- Restricted activities

Many visitors later admit they should have paid closer attention.

---

## Social Solidarity Presence

The checkpoint falls under the authority of the **Directorate of Citizen Integrity**.

Their responsibilities include:

- Identity verification
- Travel monitoring
- Population records
- Immigration review

Officers are generally courteous.

They also have access to more information than most travelers realize.

---

## SolSec Liaison Office

A permanent SolSec office operates within the checkpoint.

Official responsibilities include:

- Counterintelligence
- Counterterrorism
- Security investigations

Most travelers never interact with SolSec.

Those who do generally remember the experience.

---

## The Arrival Assessment

One of the facility's most unusual procedures.

Visitors are often asked seemingly harmless questions regarding:

- Travel plans
- Employment
- Personal interests
- Educational background

The process feels conversational.

Many travelers later discover the information was far more valuable than they realized.

---

## Processing Categories

### Green Channel

Low-risk travelers.

Fast processing.

Minimal review.

---

### Blue Channel

Routine review.

Additional verification.

Most visitors fall into this category.

---

### Silver Channel

Enhanced screening.

Usually associated with:

- Foreign government officials
- Corporate executives
- Intelligence concerns

---

### Red Channel

Restricted access.

Few travelers ever see it.

Fewer discuss it afterward.

## Public Reputation

Most Jardinese citizens appreciate the checkpoint.

They view it as:

- Professional
- Efficient
- Necessary

Foreign visitors often describe it differently.

Common observations include:

- Surprisingly polite
- Extremely thorough
- Unsettlingly organized

## Referee Notes

The Checkpoint should not feel oppressive.

It should feel inevitable.

Nobody shouts.

Nobody threatens.

Nobody waves weapons.

Instead the system quietly knows:

- Who you are.
- Where you came from.
- Why you arrived.
- Where you are going.

The true power of the checkpoint is not force.

It is information.

For most visitors, passing through the Gate is routine.

For those carrying secrets, it can be one of the most dangerous places on Jardin.
