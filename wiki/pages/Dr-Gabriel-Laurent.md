# Dr. Gabriel Laurent

*Director of Xenobiology*

## Overview

Dr. Gabriel Laurent directs Xenobiology at [[Les Jardins Fermés]] and rarely appears in public. His work inside Dome Nine — the Xenobiological Annex — has generated considerable speculation, none of it confirmed. On a world already nervous about what the Ministry of Genetics knows, a xenobiologist who never explains himself is exactly the sort of figure rumors accrete to.

## Notable For

- Runs the secretive Dome Nine xenobiology program.
- Almost never appears publicly.
- The subject of persistent, unconfirmed speculation.
