# Leo Duprey

*Supreme President of Jardin*

## Overview

Leo Duprey is the Supreme President of Jardin and the current ruler of the world, governing from [[Le Palais de Continuité]]. A former Minister of Social Solidarity, he rose through the Party apparatus over several decades and is credited by supporters with economic expansion, political stability, and greater Confederation influence — while critics, few of whom speak publicly, say he has centralized too much authority in the Presidency. He dreams of extending Jardin's reach across the entire subsector, and Minister [[Alain Mercier]]'s planned revelation threatened the stable future that ambition rests on.

## Notable For

- Head of state and Party leader of Jardin.
- Rose through Social Solidarity to the Presidency.
- His subsector ambitions depend on the stability Mercier threatened.
