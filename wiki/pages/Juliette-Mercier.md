# Juliette Mercier

*Senior Editor, Jardin Today*

## Overview

Juliette Mercier is a senior editor at Jardin Today, working out of the press district in [[Quartier Danton]], and is known for asking difficult questions. In the aftermath of Minister Mercier's assassination and the fabricated broadcast that followed it, an editor willing to press on the official story is a dangerous thing to be — and a valuable person to reach. Whether she shares the late minister's surname by blood or coincidence is not established.

## Notable For

- Senior editor known for hard questions.
- Potentially skeptical of the official Mercier narrative.
- Surname connection to Alain Mercier is unconfirmed.
