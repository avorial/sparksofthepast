        <h2>Overview</h2>
        <p>0-31 Saichi Maru is a size 5 walker robot configured for gunner and security duties. Known by the crew as Saichi, the unit combines a hardened security chassis, an Advanced TL12+ brain, integrated sensors, and mounted laser weapons into a compact patrol and shipboard defense platform.</p>
        <p>The robot is operated by Annette and is built around battlefield awareness, corridor control, and reliable response under pressure rather than social nuance or independent improvisation.</p>
