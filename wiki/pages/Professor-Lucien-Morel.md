# Professor Lucien Morel

*Chief Ecological Systems Analyst*

## Overview

Professor Lucien Morel is Chief Ecological Systems Analyst at [[Les Jardins Fermés]], where he developed several of the ecosystem-modeling theories the Gardens rely on. He believes that entire planetary biospheres can eventually be predicted mathematically, a conviction that fits neatly into a world convinced it can plan its own future. His models shape which experiments the domes pursue and which they quietly abandon.

## Notable For

- Author of influential ecosystem-modeling theories.
- Believes planetary biospheres are ultimately predictable by math.
- His models steer research priorities across the domes.
