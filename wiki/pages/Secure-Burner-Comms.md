# Secure Burner Comms

## Overview

The secure burner comms are modified cheap phones created by the supply team during [[Session 2 - Masks and Markers]]. The group bought several burners and parts, then modified them into local military-grade communicators.

## Capabilities

- Each party member received one.
- The comms use altered frequencies and hardware changes.
- Someone who does not know where the group is cannot simply listen in.
- If an enemy observes a call, they may still try to triangulate the physical location.

## Limitations

- They are improvised and local to Jardin's communication environment.
- They are not a permanent secure network.
- They do not protect against being physically followed or compromised.
