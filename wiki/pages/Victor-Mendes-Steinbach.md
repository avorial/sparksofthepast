## Profile

Victor Mendes is a sharp-minded, physically formidable Solomani Confederation citizen who spent four terms in the Confederation Navy. He left the service as a Petty Officer 1st Class after line and crew assignments marked by exploration, racial violence, and command interest in his advancement.

- Arrived without his gun, then assessed distance, cover, and possible exits as Social Solidarity and Home Guard arrived.
