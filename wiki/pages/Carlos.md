# Carlos

## Overview

Carlos is an old shipboard acquaintance mentioned when [[Cass Pien (Kolb)]] and another veteran recognized each other from service aboard the Venture. He used to run a poker game and is remembered as a cheat and poor bluffer, though still "loyal to the party" in the joking recollection.

Near the end of the session, the GM mentions "Carlos contacts" or "Carlos Contax" in connection with moving the group through the next jump once they are off Jardin. The exact name and role need human review.
