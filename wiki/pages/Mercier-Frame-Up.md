# Mercier Frame-Up

## Overview

After [[Alain Mercier]] was killed, the official narrative rapidly reframed the event as a terrorist action by genetic "half-breeds" whose genetic identities were supposedly insecure. The news story included fabricated or edited footage, including a false image of [[Zoy (Damon)]] throwing a grenade at Mercier.

The frame-up also accused the group of killing Mercier's bodyguards. Some of that claim is misleading rather than wholly invented: the group did kill Home Guard personnel during the escape, but after the situation had already turned violent and after Emma received a warning that the authorities intended to kill them.

## Why the Group Was Vulnerable

- The characters were gathered in one room despite several having prior military connections.
- They were allowed in armed because military veterans and retired service personnel may carry weapons with proper IDs.
- Several already knew one another, making it easy to portray them as an existing cell.
- Their mixed backgrounds, non-human members, and criminal or intelligence ties made them easy propaganda targets.

## Evidence and Counter-Evidence

- [[Marcel Delphine]] says the [[Jardin Syndicate]] has unedited footage.
- [[Emma Colbert (Laura)]] has a recording of the "kill them all" warning/order from her setup.
- Mercier's locked pad may contain relevant evidence.
- The public footage is known to include fake material.
