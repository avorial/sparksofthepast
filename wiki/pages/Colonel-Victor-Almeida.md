# Colonel Victor Almeida

*Commandant, Mech Combat Academy*

## Overview

Colonel Victor Almeida is Commandant of the Mech Combat Academy at the [[Fort Robespierre Naval Annex]] and one of the Confederation's foremost experts on walker warfare. A hero among mech pilots, he trains the crews who operate Jardin's combat walkers — the same class of machine the party has already brushed against in the streets of the capital. His graduates crew the war machines a rebellion would have to face.

## Notable For

- Leading Confederation authority on walker warfare.
- Trains Jardin's combat-walker crews.
- Revered among mech pilots.
