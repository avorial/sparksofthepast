# Director Simone Laurent

*Chief Administrator, Checkpoint Harmony*

## Overview

Director Simone Laurent is Chief Administrator of [[Customs & Immigration Checkpoint Harmony]], responsible for its overall operation and known for exceptional efficiency. The checkpoint is where arrivals are sorted, assessed, and — where necessary — quietly flagged, and Laurent keeps that machine running smoothly. Efficiency at a border like Harmony is not a neutral virtue: it means fewer people slip through unnoticed.

## Notable For

- Runs Jardin's principal arrival checkpoint.
- Prized for administrative efficiency.
- Her smooth operation means fewer travelers pass unscreened.
