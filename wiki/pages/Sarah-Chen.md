# Sarah Chen

*Route Intelligence Analyst*

## Overview

Sarah Chen is a Route Intelligence Analyst for [[Transstar]], tracking shipping patterns, economic trends, passenger flows, and frontier development from the company's regional data center. She often knows a recession is coming before local governments do, and [[SolSec]] frequently requests her reports — a request she pretends not to notice. Her desk is one of the best vantage points on Jardin for seeing what the numbers are about to do.

## Notable For

- Forecasts economic and shipping trends ahead of governments.
- Her reports are quietly sought by SolSec.
- A rare clear view of the subsector's moving parts.
