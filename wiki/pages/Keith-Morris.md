# Keith Morris

## Overview

Keith Morris is a corporate executive contact of [[Judith Dumont (Carla)]]. Judith hoped he could help the group get off Jardin and eventually reach [[Miasma]].

Morris explained that the immediate problem is not money for transport but identity control: the group lacks usable passports or identities after the frame-up. He suggested that leaving directly from Jardin would require passing heavy passport-control scans, and that moving elsewhere in the Jardin system first may be easier.

## Appears In

- [[Session 1 - Official Business]]

## Notes

- Morris is positively inclined toward Judith.
- He can advise on escape logistics but cannot directly bypass Jardin's identity controls.
- He may be useful once the group has forged identities or reaches a less secure departure point.
