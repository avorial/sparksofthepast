# Director-General Camille Fournier

*Head of the Ministry of Social Solidarity*

## Overview

Director-General Camille Fournier is the current head of the Ministry of [[Social Solidarity]], Jardin's internal security and social-management apparatus. A career administrator and loyal supporter of the Perfection System, she now runs the ministry that Supreme President [[Leo Duprey]] himself once led. Commanding the machinery of surveillance, assessment, and "civic harmony" makes her one of the most quietly powerful figures on the planet. (She shares only a surname with the scientists of the same name.)

## Notable For

- Runs Jardin's internal security ministry.
- Inherited the ministry Duprey once led.
- Commands surveillance, assessment, and civic-harmony operations.
