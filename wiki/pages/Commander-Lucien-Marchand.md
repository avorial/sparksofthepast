# Commander Lucien Marchand

*Director, Orbital Transfer Operations*

## Overview

Commander Lucien Marchand directs Orbital Transfer Operations at the [[Fort Robespierre Naval Annex]], controlling military movement between Jardin's surface and orbit. Few officers shape daily operations more, because nothing reaches the fleet or comes down from it without passing through his schedules. For anyone hoping to move off-world through military channels, he is both the gatekeeper and the bottleneck.

## Notable For

- Controls military surface-to-orbit movement.
- A quiet but decisive logistical chokepoint.
- Gatekeeper of any military off-world route.
