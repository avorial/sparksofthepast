# Étienne Moreau

*Regional Director, Jardin Subsector*

## Overview

Étienne Moreau is [[Transstar]]'s Regional Director for the Jardin Subsector and the senior Transstar executive on the ground. Polished, intelligent, and politically connected, he views transportation as the nervous system of civilization and genuinely believes Transstar holds the Confederation together. Some expect his next move to be a Ministry of Economics post; others are convinced he already answers to [[SolSec]]. No one is entirely certain, which is exactly how a man in his position stays useful.

## Notable For

- Senior Transstar executive in the subsector.
- Politically wired into the Confederation elite.
- Rumored to already work for SolSec.
