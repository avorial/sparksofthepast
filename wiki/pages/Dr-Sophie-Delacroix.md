# Dr. Sophie Delacroix

*Director-General of the Gardens*

## Overview

Dr. Sophie Delacroix is Director-General of [[Les Jardins Fermés]], the sealed biological research complex the Ministry of Genetics runs at the heart of Jardin's Perfection System. Regarded as one of the leading scientific minds in the Confederation, she is consulted directly by both Supreme President [[Leo Duprey]] and — until his death — Minister [[Alain Mercier]]. Overseeing the domes where Jardin's most sensitive genetic work is done places her uncomfortably close to whatever Mercier was about to reveal.

## Notable For

- Runs the Closed Gardens research complex.
- Advises the Supreme President and the late Minister of Genetics.
- Positioned near the center of the Mercier genetic controversy.
