# Dr. Luc Moreau

## Overview

Dr. Luc Moreau is a scientist or technical researcher at the [[Hall des Futurs Choisis]]. During [[Session 2 - Masks and Markers]], the party saw him leave a secured floor looking anxious and enter a public bathroom despite presumably having access to facilities on his own floor.

## Session 2

Moreau placed a data stick into a [[Mobile Data Tube]], typed a warning message on an arm computer, put the tube in the toilet, and flushed it. The message said this was the last time he would pass information, that Mercier was obviously killed for the data given to him, and that the recipient should lose his number.

Zoy and Cass recovered the tube before it escaped through the plumbing. Emma disarmed its destructive charge and preserved the data chip, which contained part of the [[Mercier Genetic Findings]].
