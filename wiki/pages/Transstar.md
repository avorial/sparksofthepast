## Overview

Transstar is the largest transportation company operating within the Solomani Confederation and the only Solomani corporation to achieve true megacorporation status. It operates freight services, passenger lines, orbital facilities, and logistics networks spanning the entire Solomani Sphere.

Officially, Transstar is a commercial enterprise.

Unofficially, it is one of the most important institutions in the Confederation.

The company provides:

* Interstellar freight transport
* Passenger transportation
* Orbital facilities management
* Frontier logistics
* Government transportation contracts
* Strategic shipping support

Many citizens joke that there are only three ways to cross the Confederation:

* Join the Navy
* Own a ship
* Ride Transstar

Most choose the third option.

## History

### Origins

Transstar began as a relatively modest transport company operating in the Solomani Rim during the final centuries of Imperial rule.

At the time, many Solomani-owned companies struggled to compete against the vast resources of Imperial megacorporations.

As support for the Solomani Movement increased, Transstar became one of its earliest and most important corporate backers.

Its executives believed that the economic interests of the Solomani worlds were being ignored by the Imperial center.

The company funded:

* Political organizations
* Party congresses
* Frontier development
* Solomani advocacy groups

These investments proved highly profitable.

---

### First Party Congress

Transstar played a major role in the First Solomani Party Congress.

The company provided discounted transportation for delegates traveling to Terra and became one of the primary financial supporters of the emerging movement.

Many historians believe the Solomani Movement would have developed much more slowly without Transstar's assistance.

---

### Rise of the Confederation

Following the creation of the Solomani Confederation, Transstar expanded rapidly.

Its existing political connections allowed it to secure:

* Government contracts
* Exclusive transportation agreements
* Infrastructure projects
* Frontier expansion initiatives

Within a century it had become the dominant merchant line in the Confederation.

---

### Modern Era

Today Transstar remains the only true Solomani megacorporation.

Its influence extends into every sector of Confederation life.

Many observers consider it less a corporation and more an unofficial ministry.

## Corporate Structure

### Board of Directors

Officially governs the corporation.

Most members possess extensive Party connections.

Many previously held government positions.

---

### Regional Directorates

Manage operations at the sector and subsector level.

These positions are highly sought after.

A successful regional director often transitions into government service.

---

### Political Liaison Office

Coordinates relations with:

* Solomani Party organizations
* Local governments
* SolSec
* Confederation ministries

Officially this office exists for regulatory compliance.

Few believe that explanation.

---

## Passenger Services

Transstar's passenger network serves billions of travelers annually.

The company has a reputation for:

* Reliability
* Punctuality
* Professionalism

It has less of a reputation for comfort.

Middle Passage accommodations are often described as:

> "Efficient."

This is rarely intended as a compliment.

Many older liners originated as:

* Colony transports
* Troop carriers
* Refitted freight vessels

especially following austerity measures after the Solomani Rim War.

---

### Taj Mahal Class

The pride of the fleet.

The Taj Mahal-class luxury liners are among the finest passenger vessels operating anywhere in Charted Space.

Features include:

* Jump-4 capability
* Luxury accommodations
* High-end dining
* Cultural programming
* VIP suites

Their service rivals anything available in the Imperium.

Their prices do as well.

## Freight Operations

Freight remains the corporation's primary source of revenue.

Transstar moves:

* Industrial goods
* Agricultural products
* Military supplies
* Consumer products
* Colonial equipment

Throughout much of the Confederation economy, if a product exists on another world, Transstar probably moved it.

## Political Influence

Transstar is deeply intertwined with the Solomani Party.

The corporation is now largely Party-owned and serves as a major source of Party income.

Benefits include:

* Access to political leaders
* Favorable contracts
* Infrastructure support

In exchange, the Party receives:

* Revenue
* Transportation capacity
* Strategic logistics

Critics frequently describe the relationship as indistinguishable from state ownership.

Supporters call it patriotic cooperation.

## SolSec Connections

Transstar is widely believed to be heavily infiltrated by SolSec.

The corporation officially denies these claims.

No one takes the denial seriously.

Passengers generally assume:

* Security personnel are present.
* Communications are monitored.
* Suspicious activities are reported.

Whether this is true varies considerably.

## Presence on Jardin

Transstar maintains a massive presence on Jardin.

Major facilities include:

### Port Sainte-Jeanne Highport

Primary orbital logistics hub.

### Rousseau Commercial Exchange

Regional corporate headquarters.

### Jardin Freight Integration Center

Coordinates cargo movement throughout the subsector.

### Hall of Commerce Annex

Maintains direct links with local government ministries.

The corporation is among the largest employers on the planet.

## Referee Notes

These ten NPCs collectively represent most of the power centers within Transstar:

* Executive Leadership
* Highport Operations
* Freight Transport
* Passenger Services
* Labor Relations
* Data Analysis
* Customs
* Luxury Travel
* Fleet Logistics
* Political Operations

Any Traveller dealing with Transstar on Jardin is likely to encounter at least one of them.

Most are loyal to Transstar first.

Some are loyal to the Party first.

A few may be loyal to something else entirely.
Transstar is effectively the economic arm of the Solomani Party.

Its executives, managers, and logistics coordinators form an unofficial pipeline into government service.

Many ministers began their careers with Transstar.

Many retired politicians end them there.

The corporation's greatest asset is not its ships.

It is information.

Every year Transstar transports billions of passengers and countless cargo shipments.

The resulting data provides a remarkably accurate picture of economic, political, and social trends throughout the Confederation.

Several intelligence analysts privately argue that Transstar understands the Confederation better than the government itself.

Whether that is true remains uncertain.

The corporation has no desire to rule.

Its leadership is content to ensure that whoever does rule depends upon Transstar to keep the Confederation moving.
