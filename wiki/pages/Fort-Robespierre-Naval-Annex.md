# Fort Robespierre Naval Annex

*"Vigilance, Duty, Victory."*

**Location:** Southern Coastal Military Zone, Jardin

**Type:** Naval Installation and Military Training Complex

**Administrative Authority:** Confederation Navy

**Operational Control:** Jardin Naval Command

**Status:** Active Military Facility

**Security Classification:** Restricted

**Population:** 850,000 Military Personnel

**Daily Population:** Approximately 1.5 Million

**Primary Functions:**
- Naval Operations
- Marine Training
- Mech Warfare Development
- Orbital Deployment
- Military Logistics
- Fleet Support

**Nickname:** The Fort

---

# Overview

Fort Robespierre Naval Annex is the military heart of Jardin.

While the public sees the Palace of Continuity as the center of political power and the Hall of Chosen Futures as the center of social planning, military officers understand that true power often resides elsewhere.

It resides at Fort Robespierre.

The Annex serves as:

- Headquarters for major naval forces assigned to Jardin
- Training center for Confederation Marines
- Primary mech warfare academy in the subsector
- Strategic logistics hub
- Surface transportation center for orbital naval facilities

Every day thousands of personnel move between the Annex and the massive military stations orbiting above Jardin.

The facility never closes.

Warships must always remain ready.

---

# History

## Origins

Fort Robespierre traces its roots to the period before Solomani intervention.

The original fortress was constructed during Jardin's expansionist era and served as headquarters for military operations against neighboring systems.

Following Jardin's defeat, the facility was seized and reorganized by Confederation authorities.

Rather than dismantle the military infrastructure, the Solomani Party expanded it.

Over time the Fort became one of the most important naval installations in the Solomani Front.

---

## The Rim War Era

During the Solomani Rim War the Annex operated continuously.

The facility trained:

- Marines
- Fleet crews
- Pilots
- Logistics specialists

Entire generations of veterans passed through its gates.

Many monuments throughout the complex commemorate units destroyed during the war.

---

## Modern Role

Today Fort Robespierre acts as both military headquarters and training institution.

The installation's influence extends throughout the subsector.

Many senior naval officers began their careers here.

---

# First Impressions

The Fort feels fundamentally different from the rest of Jardin.

Visitors encounter:

- Precision
- Discipline
- Purpose

Every movement appears organized.

Every building serves a function.

Even the landscaping feels tactical.

This is not a place designed to impress politicians.

It is designed to prepare for war.

---

# Layout

The Annex occupies hundreds of square kilometers of coastline and inland training territory.

Major districts include:

## Naval Command Citadel

The headquarters of military operations on Jardin.

Contains:

- Fleet command centers
- Strategic planning offices
- Intelligence sections
- Communications arrays

This is where regional naval operations are coordinated.

---

## Marine Training Grounds

The largest military training area on Jardin.

Facilities include:

- Urban combat zones
- Live-fire ranges
- Boarding simulators
- Atmospheric assault courses

Thousands of marines train here every year.

---

## Mech Combat Academy

One of the most famous institutions on the planet.

Responsible for:

- Walker operations
- Powered armor tactics
- Combined-arms doctrine
- Urban warfare training

Pilots from throughout the Confederation compete for admission.

Graduates are highly respected.

---

## Vehicle Development Range

Testing area for:

- Tanks
- Walkers
- Combat drones
- Experimental vehicles

New military designs are frequently evaluated here.

Explosions are common.

---

## Orbital Transfer Complex

The busiest military transportation hub on Jardin.

Serves as the primary connection between:

- Surface facilities
- Orbital stations
- Fleet anchorages

Personnel transfer continuously between the Fort and orbit.

Most naval officers spend considerable time here.

---

## Fleet Logistics Center

Handles:

- Munitions
- Spare parts
- Fuel supplies
- Vehicle maintenance

One of the largest military warehouses in the subsector.

---

# Orbital Facilities

The Annex itself does not house major warships.

Those vessels remain in orbit.

Instead the Fort serves as the surface support facility for:

## Robespierre Fleet Anchorage

Massive orbital station supporting:

- Destroyers
- Cruisers
- Fleet auxiliaries
- Assault transports

---

## Marine Orbital Barracks

Housing and deployment center for marine forces.

---

## Strategic Reserve Yards

Storage facilities containing reserve vessels and emergency supplies.

---

# Military Culture

Fort Robespierre produces officers with a distinctive reputation.

Traits commonly associated with graduates include:

- Discipline
- Aggressiveness
- Professionalism
- Political reliability

Critics occasionally add:

- Arrogance

The graduates generally do not disagree.

# Security

Security is among the strictest on Jardin.

Organizations present include:

## Naval Security Command

Primary military police force.

Responsible for:

- Internal investigations
- Base security
- Criminal enforcement

---

## Marine Provost Corps

Handles discipline among marine units.

Feared throughout the installation.

---

## SolSec Military Liaison Office

Maintains oversight regarding:

- Espionage
- Treason
- Strategic intelligence

---

## Social Solidarity Military Affairs Bureau

Ensures continued political reliability among personnel.

Its presence is often resented.

---

## Social Solidarity

# Referee Notes

Fort Robespierre should feel like the martial soul of Jardin.

The Palace governs.

The Hall plans.

The Gardens study.

Social Solidarity enforces.

Transstar enriches.

The Fort prepares for war.

This is where the Confederation's ideals become military doctrine and where the next generation of officers, marines, and mech pilots learn how to defend the Solomani Cause.

For military characters, Fort Robespierre should feel like home.

For civilians, it should feel slightly intimidating.

For enemies of Jardin, it should feel dangerous.

GM-only truth goes here.
