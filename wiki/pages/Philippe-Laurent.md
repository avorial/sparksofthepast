# Philippe Laurent

*Fleet Maintenance Director*

## Overview

Philippe Laurent is [[Transstar]]'s Fleet Maintenance Director, responsible for keeping over four hundred vessels running across the region. Famous for his obsession with preventative maintenance — "Ships don't fail. Managers fail." — he holds his engineers to a standard that terrifies them and keeps Transstar's hulls in the sky. When a Transstar ship is sound, it is because Laurent refused to let it be otherwise.

## Notable For

- Maintains a regional fleet of 400-plus vessels.
- Fanatical about preventative maintenance.
- His engineers fear disappointing him more than any inspector.
