# Étienne Marchand

*Customs Commissioner*

## Overview

Étienne Marchand is Customs Commissioner at [[Port Sainte-Jeanne Downport]] — a nightmare for smugglers and a hero to accountants. He enforces the identity and cargo controls that make leaving Jardin directly so difficult, the very controls that have stranded more than one traveler without clean papers. His bureau's scanners are the wall that any off-world escape has to get over. (He is unrelated to Chief of Staff [[Chief Helene Marchand]].)

## Notable For

- Enforces the downport's strict identity and cargo controls.
- A dreaded figure among smugglers.
- His scanners are a direct obstacle to off-world escape.
