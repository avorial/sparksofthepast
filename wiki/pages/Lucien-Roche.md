# Lucien Roche

*Information Broker*

## Overview

Lucien Roche is an information broker in [[Quartier Sainte-Liberté]] who can acquire almost anything — for the right price. In a district whose data markets trade secrets the way other places trade goods, Roche is one of the names that gets whispered when someone needs to know what the official record will not tell them. What he sells to the party across the table, he has likely already sold to two others.

## Notable For

- Deals in almost any information, for a price.
- A fixture of Sainte-Liberté's data markets.
- Loyal to the transaction, not the client.
