# Commander Sophie Durand

*Port Security Directorate*

## Overview

Commander Sophie Durand of the Port Security Directorate is a veteran investigator known for her relentless pursuit of organized crime at [[Port Sainte-Jeanne Downport]]. The downport is the [[Jardin Syndicate]]'s favorite seam — where forged manifests and quiet berths do their work — which makes Durand the standing obstacle the smugglers plan around. For a party arriving under false identity, she is precisely the wrong person to draw the attention of.

## Notable For

- Veteran anti-organized-crime investigator.
- The Syndicate's chief obstacle at the downport.
- A serious danger to travelers using forged papers.
