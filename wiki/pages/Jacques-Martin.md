# Jacques Martin

*Business Executive*

## Overview

Jacques Martin officially owns several logistics companies operating out of the Rousseau Arcology. Unofficially, he is widely rumored to maintain extensive connections within the [[Jardin Syndicate]], the kind of ties that turn a shipping firm into a discreet moving service for people and goods that would rather not be registered. Nothing has ever been proven, which on Jardin usually means either that he is careful or that he is useful to someone who is.

## Notable For

- Legitimately owns multiple logistics firms.
- Rumored to be tied into the Jardin Syndicate's movement networks.
- Never charged — a sign of caution, protection, or both.
