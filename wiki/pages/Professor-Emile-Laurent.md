# Professor Émile Laurent

*Political Theorist*

## Overview

Professor Émile Laurent is a political theorist and public intellectual based in [[Quartier Danton]], the capital's academic and congressional quarter. He appears frequently on state broadcasts, where his commentary lends scholarly weight to the Party's line. Whether he shapes official thinking or merely dresses it is a question his students debate more freely than he does.

## Notable For

- Prominent public intellectual and state-broadcast regular.
- Lends academic authority to Party positions.
- Influential in Danton's university circles.
