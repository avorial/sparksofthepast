# Amelia Torres

*Special Projects Coordinator*

## Overview

Amelia Torres is [[Transstar]]'s Special Projects Coordinator, officially overseeing strategic transportation initiatives that no one can quite define. She appears whenever [[SolSec]] becomes involved, Party officials travel, or sensitive cargo moves, and many employees quietly believe she works for intelligence services. Asked directly, she only smiles — which is answer enough for most who ask.

## Notable For

- Oversees undefined "strategic" Transstar projects.
- Surfaces around SolSec, VIPs, and sensitive cargo.
- Widely suspected of working for intelligence.
