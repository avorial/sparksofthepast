## Overview

The Ministry of Social Solidarity is the primary internal security, social administration, and compliance organization of Jardin.

## Session 1 Role

In [[Session 1 - Official Business]], [[Elena Verona]], a Social Solidarity commissar, arrived after the [[Mercier Assassination]] and attempted to contain the scene with Home Guard personnel. The public narrative later presented Verona as a harmed witness against the player characters.

The transcript clarifies that on Jardin, Social Solidarity functions as the local secret police and internal security organization. [[SolSec]] is separate and handles terrorism and external threats.

## Open Questions

- Whether Verona knowingly participated in the frame-up or was herself responding to manipulated information is not confirmed.
- The source of the "kill them all" warning/order received by Emma remains unresolved.

## Session 2 Role

During [[Session 2 - Masks and Markers]], Social Solidarity personnel blocked [[Inspector Marc Valois]] and [[SolSec]] from entering the [[Hall des Futurs Choisis]] investigation scene. Their stated argument was jurisdictional and political: [[Alain Mercier]] was a planetary official killed in a planetary government building, and a SolSec operative was implicated, so SolSec could not be trusted with evidence access.

This confirms the rivalry between Social Solidarity and SolSec is now active inside the Mercier investigation. It also gives the party a possible wedge: evidence sent to the right SolSec channel might bypass the planetary narrative, but it could also escalate the conflict between agencies.

To foreign observers it resembles a mixture of:

* Internal Security Service
* Immigration Bureau
* Social Services Agency
* Political Police
* Population Management Ministry

To most citizens of Jardin, however, Social Solidarity is simply part of daily life.

Its officers assist with:

* Citizenship updates
* Housing assignments
* Reproductive approvals
* Family placement programs
* Employment disputes
* Community mediation
* Social stability initiatives

The Ministry maintains the largest citizen records network on the planet outside the Hall of Chosen Futures.

Every citizen encounters Social Solidarity repeatedly throughout their life.

Most never consider this unusual.

## Mission

Officially:

> "To ensure the continued harmony, prosperity, and stability of the people of Jardin through guidance, protection, and responsible stewardship."

Unofficially:

Social Solidarity exists to ensure the Perfection System continues to function.

If the Hall of Chosen Futures determines what society should become, Social Solidarity ensures society remains on that path.

## History

Social Solidarity emerged following the installation of the Solomani Party government after Jardin's defeat by the Solomani Autonomous Region.

Originally intended as a conventional internal security agency, its role expanded dramatically during the rise of the Perfectionist governments.

As family structures were gradually replaced by state-managed social planning systems, Social Solidarity absorbed responsibilities previously handled by:

* Families
* Religious institutions
* Local governments
* Community organizations

By 900 the Ministry had become one of the most powerful institutions on the planet.

Today it is widely considered the second most powerful organization on Jardin after the Supreme Presidency itself.

## Organizational Structure

### Directorate of Civic Harmony

The public face of the Ministry.

Responsible for:

* Community mediation
* Social assistance
* Housing concerns
* Employment disputes
* Citizen outreach

Most citizens encounter Civic Harmony officers regularly.

These officers wear light blue uniforms and are generally well-liked.

#### Public Reputation

Helpful.

Professional.

Mildly intrusive.

---

### Directorate of Population Stewardship

Responsible for administration of:

* Reproductive approvals
* Pairing assignments
* Genetic compliance
* Child placement
* Family status records

Works closely with:

* Hall of Chosen Futures
* Ministry of Genetics

Among citizens this division is often referred to as:

> "The Matchmakers"

usually with varying degrees of affection.

#### Public Reputation

Necessary.

Unavoidable.

Sometimes feared.

---

### Directorate of Social Stability

The Ministry's investigative branch.

Responsible for:

* Political extremism
* Organized dissent
* Subversive movements
* Social unrest
* Counter-perfectionist activity

Often mistaken for SolSec by outsiders.

The Directorate does little to correct this misunderstanding.

#### Public Reputation

Polite.

Professional.

Dangerous.

---

### Directorate of Citizen Integrity

Maintains identity and compliance systems.

Responsible for:

* Identity verification
* Registry coordination
* Residency monitoring
* Immigration control
* Travel oversight

Works closely with:

* Port Sainte-Jeanne
* Hall of Chosen Futures
* Transstar

Many Syndicate investigations originate here.

#### Public Reputation

Bureaucratic.

Efficient.

Impossible to avoid.

---

### Directorate of Overseas Affairs

Responsible for Jardinese citizens living off-world.

Monitors:

* Expatriate communities
* Returning citizens
* Foreign influence
* Counter-perfectionist ideology

This division maintains offices throughout Confederation space.

It is often the first point of contact between Social Solidarity and SolSec.

#### Public Reputation

Almost invisible.

---

### Directorate of Protective Operations

The Ministry's tactical branch.

Handles:

* High-risk arrests
* Terrorism response
* VIP protection
* Riot suppression
* Strategic security operations

Members are heavily trained and equipped.

Fortunately, most citizens never encounter them.

#### Public Reputation

Trouble has already begun if they arrive.

---

## Special Divisions

### Office of Social Forecasting

Analyzes long-term social trends.

Produces reports predicting:

* Crime
* Migration
* Political instability
* Economic stress

Many of its models are considered state secrets.

Works closely with the Hall of Chosen Futures.

---

### Office of Exceptional Citizens

Officially responsible for unusual citizen cases.

Responsibilities include:

* Exceptional aptitude assessments
* Genetic anomalies
* High-value talent identification

The office's true purpose remains classified.

Rumors persist that it recruits individuals possessing unusual cognitive abilities.

---

### Office of Historical Continuity

Preserves ideological consistency across generations.

Maintains:

* Educational standards
* Historical narratives
* Public memory projects

Often involved in disputes concerning controversial historical events.

---

## Relationship with SolSec

The relationship between Social Solidarity and SolSec is often described as:

> Cooperative rivalry.

Both organizations share information.

Both investigate threats.

Both maintain intelligence capabilities.

Neither entirely trusts the other.

---

### Areas of SolSec Authority

SolSec retains jurisdiction over:

* Confederation security
* Foreign espionage
* Treason
* Military intelligence
* Interstellar threats

---

### Areas of Social Solidarity Authority

Social Solidarity controls:

* Citizen compliance
* Social planning
* Internal stability
* Population management
* Political reliability
* Immigration

---

### Common Friction Points

#### Jurisdiction

Social Solidarity often believes SolSec interferes in local matters.

SolSec often believes Social Solidarity conceals information.

#### Political Priorities

SolSec serves the Confederation.

Social Solidarity serves Jardin.

These goals do not always align.

#### Information Control

Both organizations possess enormous databases.

Both are reluctant to share everything.

---

### Typical Interaction

A SolSec agent arriving on Jardin receives full cooperation.

Provided:

* Proper credentials are presented.
* Local authority is respected.
* Appropriate notifications are filed.

Failure to follow these procedures frequently results in bureaucratic obstacles appearing with remarkable speed.

## Referee Notes

Social Solidarity should not be portrayed as cartoon villains.

Most employees genuinely believe they are helping society.

Many officers are compassionate.

Many are idealists.

Some are dangerous.

The Ministry's power comes not from fear alone but from legitimacy.

Most citizens cooperate because they believe the system works.

That belief is the true foundation of Social Solidarity's authority.

The Ministry's greatest weakness is that every aspect of Jardin depends upon it.

If Social Solidarity ever fails, the entire Perfection System may fail with it.




Secret agenda, debts, and leverage.
