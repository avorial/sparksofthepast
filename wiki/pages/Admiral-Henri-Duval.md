# Admiral Henri Duval

*Commander, Jardin Naval Command*

## Overview

Admiral Henri Duval commands Jardin Naval Command from the [[Fort Robespierre Naval Annex]] and is the senior naval officer assigned to the system. A strong advocate for expanding naval power across the Solomani Front, he sits at the point where Jardin's military ambitions meet Confederation strategy. The fleet anchored above Jardin answers, ultimately, to him.

## Notable For

- Senior naval officer in the Jardin system.
- Pushes to expand Solomani Front naval power.
- Commands the fleet stationed at Fort Robespierre.
