# Claire Bouchard

*Highport Operations Manager*

## Overview

Claire Bouchard manages Highport operations for [[Transstar]] at Port Sainte-Jeanne, coordinating thousands of arrivals and departures every week. She has a reputation for absolute efficiency: nothing surprises her, nothing delays her, and nothing intimidates her. She can reroute half a sector's shipping traffic while eating lunch — which makes her one of the most quietly indispensable people in Jardin orbit.

## Notable For

- Runs Transstar's Highport traffic at Port Sainte-Jeanne.
- Renowned for unflappable efficiency.
- Can redirect a sector's worth of shipping single-handed.
