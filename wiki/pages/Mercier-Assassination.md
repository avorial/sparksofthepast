# Mercier Assassination

## Overview

On 143-1105, Minister [[Alain Mercier]] was killed inside the [[Hall des Futurs Choisis]] shortly after the player characters were summoned there for official review. A news broadcast immediately beforehand reported that Mercier intended to reveal corruption in the genetic files at the coming Party Congress.

The killing appeared designed to implicate the gathered characters. Several characters noticed their own weapons shift, fire, or show signs of having fired without normal handling. [[Ciernan Seanislav (Peter)]] found a round missing from his shotgun and the weapon hot. Mercier was struck by multiple shots and died despite immediate attempts to help him.

## Confirmed Facts

- Mercier had publicly announced that he had found corruption in the genetic-affairs files.
- The characters were assembled in an unusually armed group at the Hall.
- Mercier passed through the office area with urgency before being shot.
- The shots were tied to or framed as coming from the characters' weapons.
- Mercier carried a locked tablet/pad and a phone.
- Mercier wore concealed armor, but the shots penetrated it.
- The public story was altered almost immediately into a terrorist narrative.

## Uncertain or Suspected

- The method used to manipulate or spoof the weapons is unknown.
- The identity of the person or group "above" Mercier is unknown.
- [[Marcel Delphine]] claims the [[Jardin Syndicate]] has unedited footage proving the characters did not shoot Mercier.
- Marcel claims Mercier's true discovery pointed upward within the system, not at Syndicate genetic fraud.
