# Pierre Gauthier

*Student Activist*

## Overview

Pierre Gauthier is a student activist and rising political figure in [[Quartier Danton]], closely watched by [[Social Solidarity]]. In a district built around the University of Jardin and the Party academies, a charismatic organizer who draws crowds is precisely the kind of person the state monitors before he becomes a movement. His trajectory could make him an ally, a martyr, or a recruit, depending on who reaches him first.

## Notable For

- A rising figure in Danton's student movement.
- Under active Social Solidarity surveillance.
- Could become an ally, an asset, or a cautionary tale.
