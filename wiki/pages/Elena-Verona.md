# Elena Verona

## Overview

Elena Verona is a commissar of [[Social Solidarity]] who arrived at the [[Hall des Futurs Choisis]] after the [[Mercier Assassination]]. The transcript first presents her over the speakers as "Elena Verona, Commissar of Social Solidarity," though later table speech also calls her Alina. The spelling should be reviewed.

Verona attempted to calm and contain the scene while Home Guard forces surrounded the characters. After Emma opened fire, Verona was wounded, grappled by [[Victor Mendes (Steinbach)]], and left unconscious during the escape.
