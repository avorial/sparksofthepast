## Contents

## Overview

Miasma is described in the transcript as a relatively low-key jump destination one jump from [[Jardin]]. [[Judith Dumont (Carla)]] considered it as a possible escape destination and hoped [[Keith Morris]] could help with plans to get there.

## Session 1 Notes

- Directly jumping from Jardin is difficult because of passport and identity controls.
- Keith Morris advised that moving to another planet within the Jardin system first may be easier than leaving directly from Jardin.
- Miasma remains a possible later destination once identity or transit problems are solved.
