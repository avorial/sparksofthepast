# Inspector-General Marc Villeneuve

*Commander, Directorate of Social Stability*

## Overview

Inspector-General Marc Villeneuve commands the Directorate of Social Stability within [[Social Solidarity]] and is widely feared by dissident organizations. His directorate is the hard edge of the ministry — the arm that identifies, contains, and removes threats to the Party's order. When the state decides that someone has become a problem, Villeneuve's people are who arrive.

## Notable For

- Leads Social Solidarity's enforcement directorate.
- Feared across Jardin's dissident networks.
- The ministry's instrument for removing threats.
