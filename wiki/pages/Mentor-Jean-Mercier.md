# Mentor Jean Mercier

*Senior Creche Mentor*

## Overview

Mentor Jean Mercier is one of the oldest serving mentors in [[Crèche District 14]]; three generations of graduates have passed through his Maison. He represents the institutional memory of Jardin's creche system, a living link between the children of today and the adults the system produced decades ago. Whether his surname connects him to the late minister is not established.

## Notable For

- Three generations of creche graduates raised in his Maison.
- Embodies the creche system's institutional memory.
- Surname link to Alain Mercier is unconfirmed.
