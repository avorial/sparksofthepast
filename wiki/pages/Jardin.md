## Overview

Jardin is the capital and dominant world of the Jardin Subsector, serving as one of the most influential political, military, and economic centers in the Solomani Front.

Originally founded in 450 by wealthy military adventurers and industrialists fleeing Imperial influence, Jardin rapidly developed into a regional power and briefly established its own colonial empire before being defeated and integrated into the Solomani movement. Over the centuries it transformed from a rebellious frontier power into one of the Confederation's most loyal and ideologically committed worlds.

Modern Jardin is governed by Supreme President Leo Duprey and the Solomani Party. The world is famous for its extensive social engineering programs, state-managed child-rearing system, government-directed reproductive matching, and centuries-long pursuit of the so-called Perfection System.

The planet's capital, **Nouvelle Sainte-Jeanne**, contains the massive Rousseau Arcology, the Hall of Chosen Futures, the Closed Gardens of the Ministry of Genetics, the Transstar Sector Exchange, and the Fort Robespierre Naval Annex.

To supporters, Jardin represents the future of humanity.

To critics, it is humanity redesigned.

## Major Cities

### Nouvelle Sainte-Jeanne
The planetary capital and largest city.

Population exceeds twelve billion.

Home to:

- [[Le Palais de Continuité]]
- Hall of Chosen Futures
- Closed Gardens
- Rousseau Arcology
- Port Sainte-Jeanne
- [[Fort Robespierre Naval Annex]]
- [[Transstar Sector Exchange]]

Most offworlders never see any other part of the planet.

---

### Nouvelle Cartier

The scientific capital of Jardin.

Home to major universities, research institutes, and Ministry of Genetics facilities.

The city's researchers are among the most respected in the Confederation.

---

### Port Liberté

The industrial heart of the world.

Produces:

- Robotics
- Starship components
- Military vehicles
- Heavy machinery

The skyline is dominated by shipyards and manufacturing towers.

---

### Cité Duprey

Administrative center of the planet.

Contains countless ministry offices, archives, civil service academies, and Party institutions.

Known throughout Jardin as:

> "The city where paperwork goes to breed."

---

### Sainte-Hélène-des-Champs

Agricultural center of the world.

Surrounded by vast engineered farmlands and experimental crop complexes.

Produces food exports throughout the subsector.

---

### Nouvelle Rousseau

A model city designed according to the principles of the Perfection System.

Every neighborhood, transit route, and public space was planned by social engineers.

Visitors either admire it or find it deeply unsettling.

---

### Port Mercier

Frontier expansion hub.

Serves as the primary departure point for:

- Colonists
- Exploration teams
- Development projects
- Frontier corporations

Possesses a more adventurous spirit than most Jardinese cities.

## Points of Interest

- **Palace of Continuity** — Seat of Supreme President Leo Duprey and the symbolic center of planetary authority.

- **Hall of Chosen Futures** — The institution responsible for citizen assignment, pairing approvals, life planning, and population management.

- **Les Jardins Fermés (The Closed Gardens)** — Headquarters of the Ministry of Genetics and the center of two centuries of social and genetic research.

- **Rousseau Arcology** — The largest population center on the planet and home to billions of citizens.

- **Transstar Sector Exchange** — Economic capital of the subsector and headquarters of Transstar's Solomani Front operations.

- **Fort Robespierre Naval Annex** — Major military complex supporting fleet operations, marine training, and combat walker programs.

- **Port Sainte-Jeanne Downport** — One of the busiest starports in the Solomani Front and the primary gateway to the world.

- **Quartier Sainte-Liberté** — Neon-lit commercial district famous for nightlife, information brokers, and Syndicate influence.

- **Crèche District 14** — One of the largest child-development complexes in the Confederation and a showcase of the Perfection System.

- **Customs & Immigration Checkpoint Harmony** — The point through which nearly every visitor enters Jardin.

## GM Truth

Most visitors believe Jardin's greatest secret is the Perfection System.

They are wrong.

The Perfection System is public.

The true secret is that after two centuries of collecting demographic, social, medical, psychological, and genetic data on billions of citizens, the Ministry of Genetics may finally possess enough information to answer questions nobody else in the Confederation can.

Questions about human behavior.

Questions about intelligence.

Questions about social stability.

Questions about the future itself.

Minister Alain Mercier's upcoming Party Congress announcement has many of the world's most powerful individuals nervous.

Some believe he is about to reveal the greatest scientific breakthrough in Confederation history.

Others fear he is about to prove that two centuries of social engineering have been based upon flawed assumptions.

Either possibility could change the future of Jardin forever.

Meanwhile, beneath the ordered surface of society, the Jardin Syndicate continues to grow in influence, Social Solidarity watches for signs of ideological contamination, and Supreme President Leo Duprey dreams of extending Jardin's influence across the entire subsector.

The experiment continues.
