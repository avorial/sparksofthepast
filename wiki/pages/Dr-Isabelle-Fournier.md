# Dr. Isabelle Fournier

*Director of Medical Botany*

## Overview

Dr. Isabelle Fournier directs Medical Botany at [[Les Jardins Fermés]] and is responsible for multiple breakthrough therapies drawn from the Gardens' living pharmacopeia. Her medical achievements have made her a popular public figure, one of the few faces of the Closed Gardens that ordinary citizens actually trust. That goodwill is politically valuable to an institution whose deeper work is anything but public.

## Notable For

- Developed several breakthrough medical therapies.
- A rare publicly trusted face of the Closed Gardens.
- Her reputation lends the Gardens political cover.
