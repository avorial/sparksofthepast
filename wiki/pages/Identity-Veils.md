# Identity Veils

## Overview

The identity veils are electronic masks or overlays provided by [[Marcel Delphine]] after the group escaped the [[Mercier Assassination]]. Marcel described them as masks that prevent camera systems from registering the wearer's normal identity.

## Known Capabilities

- Each member of the group received one veil.
- The veils last for approximately three cycles.
- Surveillance systems see a slightly altered identity rather than the wearer's real face or chassis.
- For [[0-31 Saichi Maru (Annette)]], the veil makes her appear as a similar but different robot model.

## Limits

- They are not confirmed to fool direct human recognition.
- They do not solve passport or genetic identity checks by themselves.
- They are temporary.
